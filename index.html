Why Choose Metal Braces?

Metal braces have been the gold standard in orthodontic treatment for decades. They use high-grade stainless steel brackets and wires to gradually move teeth into proper alignment. Their durability and precision make them ideal for treating a wide range of orthodontic issues, including:

  • Crowded or spaced teeth
  • Overbites and underbites
  • Jaw misalignments
  • Crossbites
